Our Story

Born from a love for nature

It all started when we witnessed beaches flooded with plastic waste and forests gradually disappearing. EcoLife was born not just as a brand, but as a wake-up call for environmental awareness.

The combination of Ocean Blue and Forest Green is our DNA. We are committed to using 100% recycled materials and dedicating 20% of our profits to planting trees and cleaning the oceans.

Our Missions

Clean The Oceans

Organizing plastic waste collection campaigns at beaches and coral reefs, restoring the blue color to our waters.

Reforestation

For every product you purchase from EcoLife, a new tree is planted in desertified areas around the globe.

Circular Economy

Manufacturing products from 100% recycled materials, zero carbon emissions, and completely eco-friendly.
